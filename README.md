# Sales Performance & Operations Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, operational efficiency, and business risks. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify operational challenges, and derive actionable insights for decision-making.

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance through key KPIs:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Profit Margin %
* Target Achievement %
* Year-over-Year Growth %

Additional visualizations include:

* Sales Trend Over Time
* Sales by Segment
* Profit by Region
* Sales by Category
* Regional Manager Performance

### 2. Operations & Risk Analysis

Focuses on operational performance and risk indicators:

* Return Rate %
* Delay Rate %
* Sales Target Monitoring
* Product Performance Analysis
* Delivery Status Distribution
* Sales by Sub-Category
* Orders Trend Analysis

### 3. Business Insights & Recommendations

Summarizes key findings from the analysis and provides strategic recommendations for business improvement.

## Key Insights

* Sales exceeded target achievement.
* Technology category generated the highest sales.
* Central region contributed the highest profit.
* Consumer segment generated the largest share of revenue.
* Operational delays and product returns remain improvement areas.
